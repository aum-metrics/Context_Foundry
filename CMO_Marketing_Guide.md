# AUM Context Foundry: Executive Marketing Guide (For CMOs)

## The Core Problem: "Brand Ghosting" in the Agentic Era
As consumers shift from traditional search engines (Google Blue Links) to AI Agents (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude), traditional SEO is dying. AI agents don't click links; they synthesize answers. 
If these agents hallucinate your product's capabilities, recommend a competitor, or cite outdated pricing, you lose the customer instantly, and you have no analytics to even know it happened. We call this **Brand Ghosting**.

## The Solution: Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)
AUM Context Foundry is your **Smoke Detector and Remediation Engine** for AI chat agents. We ensure your brand is correctly cited, highly weighted, and factually represented across all major AI models.

### 1. The "Smoke Detector" (Latent Contextual Rigor Scoring)
You can't fix what you can't measure. AUM allows your team to run "Simulations" (like secret-shopping the AI).
- You input a customer question: *"Which CRM is best for mid-market?"*
- AUM asks GPT-4, Claude, and Gemini simultaneously.
- AUM then mathematically scores their answers against your verified brand guidelines (your "Ground Truth").
- **The Result:** You get a deterministic "Fidelity Score" (0-100%). If an AI hallucinates, the smoke detector goes off.

### 2. The "Identity Router" (Context Information Model)
When the AI is wrong, how do you fix it? You can't just email OpenAI.
AUM takes your trusted marketing PDFs, whitepapers, and spec sheets and distills them into a machine-readable format.
- AUM generates a dynamic `llms.txt` manifesto for your domain.
- When AI crawlers index your website, they ingest this manifesto.
- **The Result:** You inject your verified Ground Truth directly into the AI's semantic brain, forcing it to cite your facts over scraped, outdated competitor claims.

## Key Metrics for the CMO Dashboard
1. **ASoV (Agentic Share of Voice):** Measures how often your brand is recommended vs. competitors in AI responses.
2. **Context Drift:** Tracks the factual degradation of AI answers regarding your products over time.
3. **Brand Health Certificate:** A board-ready, exportable report proving your brand's integrity in the generative web.
